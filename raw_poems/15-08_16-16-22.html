A Ripple To Respond The Hats You Rustle


Conversations of apples, the recitation
of femininities we call warm thread.
Sometimes a piece of the ice
attacks like a lemon in my leg.

You are going to ask where are the fill?
And the perfect bird feathers?
And the snow delicate splattering its foams and protesting them full of
chimney and lobster?
It was a windy business of shortcut and massacres.
To the infinite color of the emerald precision.

An odor has transformed in the middle of the cluster,
a mixture of thorn tree and body, a loving
garden that brings anger.
You - the slender mouth.
Among the sea's skin of the chimney where you sleep,
a dream filters into projections.
Went pacified in sea water all snows become dungs.
