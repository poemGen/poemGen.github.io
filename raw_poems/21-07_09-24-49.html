I Expected Daughters


They are all people
professional explications in whose domestic horses originate.

Inherit on the masks that wait for you
dismantling the molested chairs, shattering the doors.
The reasons for my respect
are perched in my hips of cork.
Aunt of the depths of my foot - your mixing
stills your delicious regard as though it were water.

Some perch but I build your brick like well.
Come with me to the dust of separations.
The heights next to hers
a story we tell in passing,
with notions of love
and a passion for journalism and oceanography
