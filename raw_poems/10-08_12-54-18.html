The Beligerance Of The Overtone Narrative


Not preserving is a form of responding.
A loop within a triangle,
the tear stained workings of arcane law.

Clotting explications and chaotic flames.
There ought to be an apple of a celestial root promising in a moonlight evening.

Multitude of stalks of cattail!
In and out of the green the transparent and the silvery
what we say continues to
fly some other giant
what a identity may teach.
