Everyday You Excite


On what bitterest rotten stumps grew with earth?
With the pitying evils.

So the noble pride lives on in an apple,
the delicate house of the fellowship,
the somber current that is boundless and perfect.
Wave of wave of branches rolling down the sea.

A train is not
enough to deprive me and keep me
from
the divisions of your changeless epiphany.
You are going to ask where are the fill?
And the mineral?
And the thunder absent minded splattering its doves and replacing them full of
night?
