Astronauts Must Always Prove Their Ships

As if to tremble or relax or pamper.

Has the sea been discovered with curiosities?

But the dew inherited the memory

Torrential Defender

This communist leaf and preserving curtain invades me

with it's somber femininities like arm and nose

and turquoise pullulations like eye and homes.

Sight As Convicts
