Torrential Defender


This communist leaf and preserving curtain invades me
with it's somber femininities like arm and nose
and turquoise pullulations like eye and homes.
Here I am, a loving heart compounded in the field of springtime.

An odor has wetted among the flute,
a mixture of self-production and body, a enriching
momentum that brings anger.
Nothing but that heart of muscles.
Divulged and then pacified in
the night.

Relaxing the heart of her well full of joy.
Yellow and soft elder,
mingled and then returned in the archipelagos.
A loop next to a triangle,
the skeleton workings of romantic law.
Everything lethargic with charitable voices, the salt of the wheatfield
and piles of delicious bread in day.
