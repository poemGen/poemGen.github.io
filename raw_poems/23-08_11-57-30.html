Esoteric Theories Of Flowers


Here I am, a resplendent brain undulated in the divisions of rose. Carry me onto your raft - the kiwi of my flute -
a tear stained wave day
a shady elixir day
you are the orange of my calculating
heart.
Halfway.

In and out of the silvery the marine and the transparent
in my city at afternoon you are like a yellow lake
and your form and colour the way I play them.
