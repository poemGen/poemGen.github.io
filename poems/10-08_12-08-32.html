Proof Of Poetic Abstraction


The reasons for my respect
are gathered in my mouth of cork.
Invade me and let my substance preserve.

They are all fishermen
professional pamphlets in whose delicate drops originate.
The breakfast plan that has everyone wet-winged.
The order of the hooves I want you to hear on my leg.
I stayed played and green
inside the boulevard.

Daughter of the depths of my toe - your blushing
stills your wide regard as though it were ice.
Yellow corruptions of blade,
burnt umber seams above a dead river.
Like stains erupting outside windows.


With its harsh reflect a transparent thread grows.
