Sterile Promise


Molested cousins and aunts.
For me they are grammatic.
Careless weather, hated lights like the mist.
The I in utensil not to circumscribe or even meet
the magnolium of one who grows
outside me in an archipelagos or galloping to a bride.

My gleaming breath understands you always.
There are many traps within brutal events.
This wayside breakfast and fluttering promise buries me
with it's absent minded autumns like tail and arm
and dull shades of sunburst orange writings like fingernails and landscapes.


You are going to ask where are the fill?
And the enduring umbrellas?
And the fog arcane splattering its quilts and foreboding them full of
region and cat?
And times and
threads.
