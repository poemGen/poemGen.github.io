Every Woman Is A Camera


Multitude of wells!
In and out of the blue the sunburst orange and the sepia
there are no shrapnels but difficult cycles of old warrior's medal and sand-colored
femininities of electrical boney sand.
You flow in the university as in a solute city.
Transforming a juice
blossomed in the decisive sunshine.

A loaf of bread baked with hated joy and salt.
What replaces the props of sincerity?

Silvery howls of legume,
sand-colored seams above a brutal school.
For flag was hollow and morally neutral.
Amid the drowning billows of ultraviolet smoke.
Has the thicket been created with mysteries?
