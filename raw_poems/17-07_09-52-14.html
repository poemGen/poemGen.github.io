The Delirious Pioneer


Pockets of steel converted into fused quartz.
And defenders and knaves.
What is this synonym but a memory dismantled of its promises?
Felicity is gone, the subject has developed.

Like corpses executing next to serendipities.
The land outside hers
a tale we speak in passing,
with notions of pride
and a passion for computer
science and computer science
forming a ribbon
rescued in the resolute sunshine.

To the loving blazing wreath a delirious echo day
carry me onto your raft - the lemon of my precision -
what coddles the props of purity?

What is this inscription but a memory congealed of its leaves?
Once there was a smothered sailor who
upgraded at parties, sitting in a line segment, among forests.
